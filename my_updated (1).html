Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?
It depends on the package. A Landing Page (Starter) takes about 7 days, a Business Website around 14 days, and a Full Digital Presence up to 3 weeks. Timelines can be shorter depending on how quickly you provide content and feedback.
Do you offer revisions after the website is delivered?
Yes. Every package includes revision rounds during the build. The Premium package also comes with 1 month of post-delivery support for any tweaks or updates you need.
What do I need to provide before you start building?
Ideally your logo (if you have one), brand colours, text content, and any photos you want used. If you don't have these ready, I can help guide you through what's needed — don't let that stop you from reaching out.
Can you teach French online or only in person?
Both. Sessions can be conducted online via video call or in person depending on your location and preference. Online sessions are just as effective and more flexible for busy schedules.
How do counselling sessions work?
Sessions are private, confidential, and non-judgmental. You book a time via WhatsApp, and we meet either in person or online. The first session is usually about understanding your situation and what you want to work on together.
How do I pay for your services?
Payment is via M-Pesa or bank transfer. A 50% deposit is required before work begins, with the remaining balance due on delivery. Full payment terms are discussed when you get in touch.
Can you handle projects outside Kenya?
Absolutely. Web development, French services, and counselling can all be done remotely. I work with clients across Africa and beyond. Just reach out and we'll figure out the best way to work together.
